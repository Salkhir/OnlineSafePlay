💡 Why this works — and a tip before you start

  • Agreements children help create are far more likely to be followed than rules handed down from above.
  • Fill this in together — ideally with your child in the room. Ask their input on the limits before you set them.
  • Once done, print it, sign it together, and put it somewhere visible — the fridge, their bedroom door, or next to the TV.
  • Revisit it every 6 months as your child grows — what works at 10 won't work at 13.
